Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Image Compressor. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

Images You Upload:

  • When you use our service, you upload image files for compression
  • We temporarily process these images through our server and the Tinify API
  • We do not store your images permanently on our servers

Technical Data:

  • File metadata (filename, file size, format)
  • Server logs for error tracking and service improvement
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your images solely for the following purposes:

  • Compression: Processing your images through the Tinify API to reduce file size
  • Format Conversion: Converting images to your selected format (JPEG, PNG, WebP, AVIF)
  • Service Delivery: Returning the compressed images to you

We do NOT:

  • Store your images permanently
  • Use your images for training AI models
  • Share your images with third parties (except Tinify for processing)
  • Analyze the content of your images

4. Third-Party Services

Tinify API:

We use the Tinify API (TinyPNG/TinyJPG) for image compression. When you upload an image, it is sent to Tinify's servers for processing. Tinify's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing Tinify's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • Temporary Storage: Images are temporarily stored in memory during processing
  • Automatic Deletion: Images are automatically deleted after download or when the session ends
  • Server Logs: Technical logs are retained for up to 30 days for debugging and security purposes

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data during transmission and processing:

  • HTTPS encryption for data transmission (when deployed with SSL)
  • Secure API communication with Tinify
  • No permanent storage of user images
  • Regular security reviews and updates

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we process
  • Deletion: Images are automatically deleted after processing
  • Objection: Stop using the service at any time
  • Data Portability: Download your compressed images

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. International Data Transfers

Your images may be processed on servers located in different countries depending on Tinify's infrastructure. By using our service, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
